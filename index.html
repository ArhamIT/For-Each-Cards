Mango

It is a pulpy, juicy fruit with a sweet taste. It has one big seed in the centre, which we cannot eat.

Apple

Apple is a sweet and delicious fruit that is very healthy. It is also a good disease-fighter and we can eat it every day.

Bananas

This fruit is a rich source of vitamins, minerals and packed with nutrients. Banana is extensively grown in tropical and subtropical countries.

Strawberry

red, heart-shaped fruits. They are eaten fresh, often with cream.

Pine Apple

Pineapples grow on plants that have leaves shaped like long swords. The plants also have purple flowers and smaller leaves.

Oranges

Oranges are a very good source of Vitamin C. Orange juice is an important part of many people's breakfast.
